Tide

Tide is a desktop app designed for swimmers who want to take the videos on their Watch Later list into the water as audio.

Paste a video link to convert it into swim-ready audio and save it locally. When a compatible earpiece is connected, Tide seamlessly transitions into a dedicated sync experience, using flowing water, subtle motion, and intuitive folder interactions to make the transfer feel tangible and intuitive.

Designed as a cinematic product concept, Tide combines a clean, editorial interface with responsive water-based transitions, transforming a practical file management workflow into a calm, immersive pre-swim ritual.
